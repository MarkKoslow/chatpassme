We are looking for a select number of highly motivated high school students looking for real world experience in a fast paced Silicon Valley start-up.

This summer we are offering a select number of students who will be in grades 9-11 for the school year starting in Fall 2013 to work as Chatpass.me interns in your school. This is a competitive internship and only a few students will be chosen to participate.

If you meet all the requirements, Chatpass.me an internship will guarantee you an awesome summer job to boost that college resume, and put you on a path to a great gig in technology and social media.

Responsibilities

Interested? Here are the deets on the internship:
  • Weekly check in with the Chatpass.me team or mentor
  • Email updates on progress made in user acquisition
  • Contribute content daily in the form of whistles
  • Test the app's virality
  • Provide feedback from your user base to the Chatpass.me HQ
  • Create and evaluate new content that will appear in the Chatpass.me network, on our Tumblr, Facebook and Twitter accounts

Perks!

  • You have the opportunity to be featured in our social media and blog
  • Upon successful completion of the internship, we will provide you with a letter of recommendation validating your time and contributions to building the Chatpass.me community
  • You must be able to attract 5 new users to download and register for the app to be eligible for the internship
  • Must have a strong personality, yet capable of working within a team towards a group goal
  • Humor is a plus, entertaining HQ while accomplishing these tasks will lead to massive brownie points
  • You will be assigned to a team, the captain of which will be one of our recent college grads, or a student currently enrolled in a top tier university.
